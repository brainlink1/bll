What is BrainLink?

BrainLink is Algeria's premier business and engineering innovation competition, designed to discover and nurture the next generation of strategic thinkers and technical innovators.

Innovation Challenge

Tackle real-world business problems through innovative engineering solutions and strategic thinking.

Skill Development

Enhance your strategic thinking, technical engineering skills, problem-solving abilities, and collaborative teamwork.

Network Building

Connect with industry leaders, fellow innovators, business strategists, engineers, and potential employers.

Career Acceleration

Open doors to exceptional opportunities with leading organizations in both business and engineering sectors.

Business strategy provides the vision. Engineering provides the solution.

We believe that the future belongs to those who can bridge the gap between business strategy and engineering innovation. BrainLink is your platform to prove you're that future.

Your Journey to Innovation Excellence

Two Transformative Phases, One Extraordinary Goal

Phase 1: Digital Qualifier

Format: 100% Online Competition

Compete from anywhere in Algeria. Teams will receive a comprehensive business case requiring innovative engineering solutions. Submit your strategic proposals, technical frameworks, and implementation plans through our digital platform.

Duration: 2 weeks intensive challenge

Outcome: Top teams advance to Finals

Phase 2: The Grand Finale

Format: On-Site at Henkel Algeria

Selected finalists will experience an immersive innovation sprint at Henkel Algeria's facilities. Engage directly with business leaders and engineering experts, refine your solutions with hands-on guidance, and present to a distinguished panel of judges in person.

Duration: Full-day intensive experience

Outcome: Winners announced & prizes awarded
